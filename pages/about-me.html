About Me

I am a junior Computer Science major at the University of Notre Dame with an interest in artificial intelligence. At Notre Dame, I live in Fisher Hall, which is definitely the best dorm on campus (if you ignore aesthetics). I'm from the Washington DC area and went to high school at Gonzaga. When I'm not working, I like to run and play golf and am a huge Notre Dame and DC sports fan (particularly of the Nationals and Capitals). I'm also an avid reader of Nate Silver's FiveThirtyEight blog focused on sports and politics through the lens of data analytics.
